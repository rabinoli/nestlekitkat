About KitKat Chocolates

KitKat is a globally popular chocolate-covered wafer bar confection that originated in the United Kingdom in 1935, created by Rowntree's of York. Now produced by Nestl√© worldwide, KitKat consists of layers of crispy wafer encased in smooth milk chocolate. It is well-known for its distinctive shape, with bars that can be easily snapped apart into individual fingers. KitKat has a variety of flavors and types, catering to diverse tastes and regional preferences. Its catchy slogan, "Have a break, have a KitKat," emphasizes its role as a convenient and enjoyable snack.

KitKat Original

KitKat Original is a popular chocolate-covered wafer bar, featuring layers of crisp wafer coated in milk chocolate. Known for its distinct taste and iconic red packaging, each bar is divided into finger-like segments for easy sharing. The slogan, "Have a break, have a KitKat," highlights it as a perfect snack for a quick break.

KitKat Dark

KitKat Dark is a variation of the classic KitKat bar, featuring layers of crisp wafer covered in rich dark chocolate. It offers a more intense chocolate flavor compared to the original. Like the original, KitKat Dark is divided into finger-like segments, making it easy to share and enjoy.
